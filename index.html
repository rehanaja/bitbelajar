About

BitBelajar is a collaborative online learning community built by students, for students and young learners who want to grow together in technology and self-development.

We believe that every small step (bit) in learning has a big meaning if done together. Therefore, BitBelajar is here as an open space for anyone who wants to learn without feeling alone, without fear of being left behind.
